# REDESIGN BRIEF - Complete UI Overhaul

## Goal
Each template must look like it was designed by a boutique web agency, NOT generated by AI. Each should feel like a real business website for that specific industry. No generic card layouts. No "AI template" vibes.

## Design Principles
- **Clean & minimalistic** — lots of whitespace, restrained color palettes
- **Industry-authentic** — each template should match what top businesses in that industry actually look like
- **Subtle animations** — CSS only, no framer-motion. Fade-ins, gentle parallax, smooth hovers. Nothing flashy.
- **Typography-driven** — let fonts do the heavy lifting. Mix serif headings with sans-serif body for elegance.
- **Meticulous details** — consistent spacing, aligned grids, proper visual hierarchy

## DO NOT USE framer-motion. Use Tailwind CSS animations and @keyframes only.

## Template 1: Dental Office — "Valley Creek Dental Care"
**Vibe:** Warm, welcoming, trustworthy. Think of sites like aspendentalgroup.com or tenderdental.com.
- Soft color palette: warm whites, light blues, hint of teal
- Hero: Large, warm headline ("Your Smile, Our Passion"), subtle gradient overlay, prominent "Book Appointment" button
- **Layout:**
  - Full-width hero with a welcoming tagline
  - "Why Choose Us" section with 3 clean icon cards (gentle hover lift)
  - Services section — simple grid with service name + short description, no cards with heavy shadows
  - Meet the team — circular headshots (use placeholder initials) with name + role
  - Patient testimonials — simple quote cards, one at a time, with quotation marks
  - Insurance/payment info — clean list, not a cluttered grid
  - Footer with contact info, map placeholder, hours
- **Font:** Serif headings (Georgia or similar), clean sans-serif body

## Template 2: HVAC Company — "Harris Air Services"
**Vibe:** Reliable, urgent when needed, family-owned. Think onehourheatandair.com.
- Color palette: Deep navy + bright orange accents + white
- Hero: Bold headline ("McKinney's Trusted HVAC Experts Since 2008"), phone number prominently displayed, "Schedule Service" CTA
- **Layout:**
  - Hero with urgency strip at top ("24/7 Emergency Service — Call Now")
  - Trust bar: "Licensed • Insured • Family-Owned • 15+ Years Experience"
  - Services section — clean 2-column layout with icon + title + description
  - "Why Harris Air" — 3 big stats (Years in Business, Happy Customers, 5-Star Reviews)
  - Service area list — clean text, not a fake map
  - Testimonials — star ratings + quotes
  - Emergency CTA banner — full-width, dark background, "AC Emergency? Call (469) 325-1034"
  - Footer with contact, hours, license numbers
- **Font:** Bold sans-serif headings, clean body text

## Template 3: Med Spa — "Serenity Med Spa"
**Vibe:** Luxurious, calming, premium. Think of high-end spa sites with lots of white space.
- Color palette: Soft blush/rose gold + cream/off-white + charcoal text
- Hero: Elegant headline ("Reveal Your Natural Beauty"), minimalist, lots of breathing room
- **Layout:**
  - Full-width hero with subtle gradient, centered text, CTA "Book Consultation"
  - Featured treatments — horizontal scroll or 3-column with treatment name + starting price
  - "The Serenity Experience" — short paragraph about the spa's philosophy
  - Treatment menu — clean categorized list with prices (Injectables, Skin, Body, etc.)
  - Before/After placeholder section — simple side-by-side boxes
  - Testimonial — single large quote, elegant typography
  - "Meet Dr. [Name]" — photo placeholder + bio
  - Footer — minimal, elegant
- **Font:** Serif headings (think Playfair Display style — use Georgia), light sans-serif body
- **Special:** Use thin borders, subtle line separators, rose-gold/blush accent colors

## Template 4: Law Firm — "Stone & Associates"
**Vibe:** Authoritative, professional, approachable. Think top Dallas law firms.
- Color palette: Dark navy + gold/amber accents + white
- Hero: "Protecting What Matters Most" — strong, confident, clean
- **Layout:**
  - Hero with dark overlay, strong CTA "Free Consultation"
  - Practice areas — icon + title grid (Personal Injury, Family Law, Business Law, Criminal Defense)
  - Results/stats bar — "$50M+ Recovered • 500+ Cases Won • 20+ Years Experience"
  - Attorney profiles — professional layout, name + title + brief bio
  - Client testimonials — formal, with case type tags
  - "Why Stone & Associates" — 3-4 differentiators in clean layout
  - Contact section — phone, email, address, "Schedule Your Free Consultation" form placeholder
  - Footer — professional, links to practice areas
- **Font:** Classic serif headings, professional sans-serif body
